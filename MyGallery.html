Books

Books are written works of literature that provide information, tell stories, or explore ideas. They can be in various genres, including fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and more. Authors are the individuals who create these books, putting their thoughts, experiences, and imaginations into words. They have a unique ability to captivate readers with their storytelling skills, evoke emotions, and provoke deep thoughts
